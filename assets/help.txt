Welcome to ReConnect!

Helping you manage your PC screen time effectively.

Getting Started:

1. Explore Your Homepage
    Here, you can view and analyze your screen time data in various formats.
        Weekly Analysis: Track your screen time on a weekly basis.
        Application Insights: Identify which applications consume most of your time.
        Browser Usage: See detailed reports on your time spent on different browser tabs.

2. Navigate the Action Bar
    Located at the top of your screen, the action bar offers two main options:
        Screen Time: View detailed screen time statistics and insights.
        Focus: Set timers to enhance your productivity. (Note: This feature is still under development)

We hope you find ReConnect helpful in managing your screen time!
